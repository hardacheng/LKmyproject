#FEAR OF MISSING OUT#

RANDOMEVENT SS20

Randomevent SS20 将目光聚焦于青年人的社交状态。社交的方式从古至今一直在进化着，而在如今信息交流如此迅速多元的年代，青年人如何面对和处理自我的社交关系？

本季以「Fear of missing out（FOMO）」为题，通过表现青年人在社交中害怕错失结交社交关系、渴望新体验的心态，而产生的一种恐惧、焦虑的状态。希望能引起青年人对于自我社交方式的思考。

设计中主要融入了「模糊扩散」的手法来传达出焦虑、患得患失的不确定感，从而传达出本季的精神。同时融合蟒纹、条纹、格子等经典元素，传达出 Randomevent 一贯对于面料及图案应用的理解

Randomevent SS20 focuses on the social status of young people. The way of social communication has been evolving since ancient times. In the era of information exchange so fast and diverse, how do young people face and deal with their social relations?

The title of this season is "Fear of missing out (FOMO)". It is a state of fear and anxiety created by expressing the mentality of young people in social situations that they are afraid of missing social relationships and yearning for new experiences. Randomevent hopes to arouse young people's thinking about self-social ways

The design incorporates "fuzzy diffusion" to convey anxiety, gains and losses of uncertainty, and thus convey the spirit of the season. At the same time, classic elements such as python patterns, stripes, and lattices are blended to convey Randomevent's consistent understanding of fabric and pattern applications.
